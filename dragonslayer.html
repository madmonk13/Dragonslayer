Your daughter Lyra has been chosen in the village lottery to be sacrificed to the dragon Pyraxis. You have 30 days to grow strong enough to defeat him and save your daughter.

📖 How to Fight

The Basics

Combat is driven by d6 dice. Every 6 points of Combat stat gives you one die. You choose how many dice to roll on your attack — unused dice automatically roll to block enemy damage. Splitting your dice between attack and defense is the core decision every turn.

Stamina & Dice

Your stamina limits how many dice you can use. Available dice = min(Combat dice, floor(Stamina ÷ 6)). At full stamina you can use all your dice. Drain it on heavy attacks and both your offense and defense suffer.

🎲×1 ⚔️ is a light attack — it restores stamina (+6) and costs nothing.
🎲×2+ ⚔️ are heavy attacks — each die costs 6 stamina.
Blocking

After your attack, the enemy hits back. Any dice you didn't use for attacking are rolled — their sum reduces incoming damage. Your armor adds a flat block on top. Example: you have 3 dice, attack with 2 → the 3rd die rolls (say a 4) → enemy damage reduced by 4, then by your armor.

Defend & Potion

🛡️ Defend — skip your attack, restore 12 stamina, and absorb up to 12 damage from the enemy's hit. Use this when stamina is low to recover your dice pool.

🧪 Potion — drink a health potion mid-combat. The enemy gets a free attack while you drink.

Damage Formula

Your damage: dice sum − enemy DEF block. Minimum 1.
Enemy damage: dice sum − your block dice roll − your armor block. Minimum 0.

Enemies also have DEF — high-DEF enemies eat into your dice rolls. Upgrade your Combat stat or buy better weapons to roll more dice and overpower their defence.
